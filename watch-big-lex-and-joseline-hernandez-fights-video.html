    Big Lex and Joseline Hernandez Fights video has been trending on social media.

    At the Floyd Mayweather vs. John Gotti III exhibition boxing bout, a recent altercation occurred involving reality TV personalities Big Lex and Joseline Hernandez. The fight took place on Sunday at the FLA Live Arena in Sunrise, Florida.

    Mayweather, a renowned boxer, was pitted against John Gotti III, the grandson of a notable New York mobster.

    In the midst of the eighth fight, referee Kenny Bayless disqualified Gotti III for inappropriate grabbing, taking action midway through the sixth round.

    Following the final bell, Gotti aggressively pushed the referee aside and charged toward Floyd Mayweather, causing a chaotic scene inside the ring.

    Both fighters encouraged the commotion, intensifying the atmosphere.

    Big Lex and Joseline Hernandez Fights video

    While the incident spilled over backstage, it remains unclear whether the altercation involving Joseline Hernandez and Big Lex was connected to the earlier-mentioned fight between Floyd Mayweather and John Gotti III.

    Both Hernandez and Lex are reality TV personalities. Witnesses observed Hernandez subjecting Lex to severe physical aggression, resulting in Lex losing his composure during the altercation.

    Shockingly, Hernandez even applied a chokehold on Lex, while bystanders remained passive without intervening to stop the situation.

    After being momentarily released from Joseline Hernandez’s grip, Big Lex found herself targeted once again as Hernandez returned to kick her while she was on the ground.

    In an attempt to intervene, Hernandez appeared to strike security guards repeatedly.

    Despite Lex managing to escape the immediate vicinity, Hernandez and her friends gave chase and eventually caught up with her.

    When confronted, Lex responded by throwing her coffee and striking Hernandez with her heels.

    Joseline Hernandez is widely recognized as the star of the reality television program “Joseline’s Cabaret,” which premiered on the Zeus Network on January 19, 2020.

    The show has aired three seasons thus far, with Big Lex making an appearance in the second season.

    The ongoing controversy surrounding this altercation adds to the drama and intrigue associated with these reality TV personalities.
