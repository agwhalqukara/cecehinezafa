    Italia Anita Maria Smith was Born 32 Years Ago

    Italia Anita Maria Smith was born on January 3, 1991, in the United States and is currently 32 years old. Her father is famous American musician LL Cool J, while her mother is Simone Johnson, an entrepreneur, jewelry designer, and philanthropist. She has 3 siblings and they all enjoy a close relationship with their parents and each other.

    Italia had a luxurious childhood as her father was already a celebrity at the time of her birth. She was showered with love and attention by her parents and had everything she needed. She attended Manhasset Senior High school and after she graduated in 2009, she enrolled at Northeastern University where she graduated with a degree in Business Administration.

    Meet Italia Anita Maria Smith’s Parents

    Italia Anita Maria Smith’s parents are LL Cool J and Simone Johnson. The couple met in the early 80s as teenagers during matchmaking by one of their mutual friends. They dated for several years before getting married on August 7, 1995.

    They have remained happily married for 25 years and in 2020, Simone threw a surprise party to celebrate his recognition by the Kennedy Center Awards. The celebrity couple often upload posts to appreciate each other on their social media accounts.

    Italia Anita Maria Smith’s Father is an Accomplished Musician

    Italia’s father James Todd Smith who is more commonly known as LL Cool J has built a successful career as a musician. He achieved his breakthrough in the music world after he released his single I Need a Beat in 1985.

    He has released 13 studio albums and has won the Grammy Award twice. LL Cool J has ventured into acting and these ventures contributed to his estimated net worth of $120 million.

    Italia Anita Maria Smith’s Mother Battled a Bone Cancer

    In 2004, Italia’s mother, Simone was diagnosed with a rare bone cancer called chondrosarcoma. She went through 15 hours of surgery and took two and a half years to recover. Her husband was very supportive during that period and she referred to him as “the rock”.

    Simone is now cancer-free and to support others battling cancer, she donates a portion of the proceeds from her jewelry store to the American Cancer Society through the “Beat Cancer like a Boss” campaign.

    Who are Italia Anita Maria Smith’s Siblings?

    Italia Anita Maria Smith has three siblings, an elder brother named Najee Laurent Todd Eugene Smith and two younger sisters named Samaria Leah Wisdom Smith and Nina Simone Smith. The Smith siblings enjoy a very close relationship with each other. Meet Italia’s siblings below.

    Najee Laurent Todd Eugene Smith is Italia’s Eldest Sibling

    Najee Laurent Todd Eugene Smith is LL Cool J’s first child and he was born on September 18, 1989. He graduated with a degree in Photography from Long Island University and currently works as a photographer and videographer.

    In 2015, Najee was arrested for fighting with security guards outside a restaurant in New York, although the charges were dropped later that year. He is neither married nor in any known relationship.

    Samaria Leah Wisdom Smith was Born in 1995

    Samaria Leah Wisdom Smith was born on September 15, 1995. She attended Campbell Hall High School and Fashion Institute of Technology (FIT) where she obtained two degrees in Fashion Design and Fashion Merchandising.

    Samaria is a fashion and fitness model and also an Instagram Influencer. She posts pictures of her work on her Instagram account @samarialeah and Twitter @Samaria_Leah where she has thousands of followers. She is currently in a relationship with her boyfriend Jordan Fuller.

    Nina Simone Smith is Italia’s Youngest Sibling

    Nina Simone Smith Is Italia’s youngest sibling. She was born on August 9, 2000, and is popularly known as @ninabandzz across her social media accounts. Nina is the family’s sweetheart and she appeared on an episode of Oprah Winfrey’s Next Chapter alongside her parents.

    Is Italia Anita Maria Smith Married?

    Italia Anita Maria Smith is married to her husband Lamar Cardinez. The duo met during a trip to Nairobi in 2013 and instantly fell in love. They started dating each other and in November 2017, they got engaged.

    Almost two years later, Italia and Lamar got married in a lavish ceremony on July 17, 2017. Their wedding took place at the Sparkling Pointe Vineyards in New York and had a lot of celebrities in attendance.

    Italia Anita Maria Smith and her husband Lamar Cardinez have remained happily married and have had no scandal trailing their marriage. She frequently shares happy pictures of them on her Instagram account @italiaanitasmith.

    She Shares Two Kids with her Husband

    Italia Anita Maria Smith has two children with her husband Lamar Cardinez. Their first child and son King Cardinez was born on March 14, 2018, while they welcomed their daughter Faith Cardinez on September 23, 2020.

    What Does LL Cool J’s Daughter do for a Living?

    Italia Anita Maria Smith is a Real Estate Agent. She obtained a certificate in Commercial Real Estate from Cornell University in 2019 and has effectively used her certificate to build her career. She currently works at Keller Williams Realty, Inc.
