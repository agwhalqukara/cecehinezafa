    Talk about a star-studded double date!

    This past weekend, Jennifer Aniston and Justin Theroux reportedly enjoyed a night out with pal Sandra Bullock and her new boyfriend, Bryan Randall, in Austin, Texas.

    According to People, the famous foursome dined at Bess Bistro on Saturday, Sept. 5 and celebrated the occasion like “close, fond friends.”

    Jennifer and her husband, Justin.

    “It was a nice intimate setting and the couples were very cozy,” onlooker Rhonda Scott told the magazine of the couples. “They seemed to know each other very well, and seemed quite close.”
