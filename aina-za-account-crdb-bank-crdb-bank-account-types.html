    • Kuhusu CRDB ( About CRDB Bank)

    CRDB Bank Plc is a leading African bank and financial services provider in Tanzania with a current presence in Tanzania, Burundi, and East Africa.

    The bank was incorporated in 1996 and was listed on the Dar es Salaam Stock Exchange in June 2009. Over the years, CRDB Bank has grown to become the most innovative and preferred financial services partner in the region. Backed by a strong portfolio and uniquely designed products, CRDB Bank remains the most responsive bank in the region.

    CRDB offers a comprehensive range of corporate, retail, business, treasury, Premier, and wholesale microfinance services through a network of 260 branches, 551 ATMs, 18 deposit machines, 12 mobile branches, and 1184 Point of Sale (POS). Similarly, the bank has dozens of partners and microfinance institutions through which related services are provided to all clients.

    Currently, CRDB has 450 partner institutions in microfinance. CRDB Bank was the first to offer agency banking in Tanzania at the beginning of 2013 and we now have 3,286 FahariHuduma agents across the country. The bank also operates through online and mobile banking.
